Credit subscriptions, White Label subscriptions and legacy plans automatically renew every billing period until you cancel or schedule an end date in advance. You may cancel your 8th Wall plan at any time by logging into your 8th Wall account and going to the “Account” page of your workspace.

If you are canceling while on a paid legacy plan, you will have access to legacy features through the end of your billing period. After your plan cancels, you will be on a free 8th Wall account.

There are no prorations or refunds for partial or unused months or credits.

To schedule a White Label subscription end date in advance, please update the Project Duration settings for your project ([instructions can be found here](http://8th.io/subscription-duration)). When a White Label subscription ends, the project will be archived so it can no longer be viewed. This applies to both 8th Wall Hosted and App Key (formerly known as “Self-Hosted”) projects. A project can be reactivated with a new White Label subscription or by launching the project with 8th Wall branding.
